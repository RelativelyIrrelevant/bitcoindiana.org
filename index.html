Bitcoin Accepting Merchants

A circular economy is where bitcoin flows from earners to merchants and back again—closing the loop so saving in bitcoin doesn’t require exiting to fiat just to live. The more we spend bitcoin for real goods and services (as a medium of exchange), the more resilient local communities become: fewer conversion points, lower friction, and stronger incentives for merchants to stack and hold.
